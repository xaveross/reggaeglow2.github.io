ABOUT US

Reggae Glow Cosmetics is a company that focuses on providing their customers with essential products used for the hair and skin. Our company guarantees clients with a sense of confidence, as our main focus is embracing authenticity and our true self as Jamaicans. We strive to cater towards using self care products made from various herbs and natural ingredients, inspired by our country’s ancient beauty traditions, as we recognize the benefits of organic mixtures, and how they impact our overall physical health and lifestyle.

OurMission

Our mission as a company is to educate youth on the importance of loving and caring for oneself. We encourage young Jamaicans to cherish and practise our country’s beauty traditions, through our ancestral traditions of Jamaica. Hence, our job is to promote self care products, for Jamaicans, as glowing skin and hair maintenance is a priority for the sake of our presentability and mental health.
